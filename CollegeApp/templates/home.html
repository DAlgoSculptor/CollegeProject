Frequently Asked Questions

What is NutriScan?

NutriScan is an advanced tool designed to help you make healthier food choices by providing detailed ingredient analysis of the products you consume.

How does the scanning feature work?

You simply upload an image of the food product, and NutriScan analyzes the ingredients to provide you with information about harmful chemicals and nutritional content.

Is NutriScan free to use?

NutriScan offers both free and premium options. The free version provides basic functionality, while the premium version offers additional features and more detailed analysis.

How can I contact support?

You can reach out to our support team via the Contact page on our website or email us directly at support@nutriscan.com.
